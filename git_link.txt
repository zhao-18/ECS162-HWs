 features:
  Login by pressing the Log in button on right top
  Log out by pressing the log out button on right top when logged in
  Pull up the comment pane by clicking one of the comment button
  Comment on article by typing in content in the field with "Share your thoughts." placeholder and press enter
  Reply to a comment by clicking on Reply link below comment, type in the comment and press enter

  Moderator:
   Moderate comment by pressing Delete link below comment
   Moderate word by clicking on the word or moderate section of comment by highlighting the part you wish to be deleted
